## About Our Icarus Server

Welcome to Sheltered Gaming's Icarus server! Explore a vast wilderness filled with dangers, treasures, and incredible discoveries. Whether you prefer PvE exploration or cooperative challenges, Icarus offers unforgettable adventures.

### Server Features

- **Cooperative Gameplay** - Work together with friends to survive and thrive
- **Rich Resource System** - Hunt, gather, farm, and craft your way to success
- **Dynamic Weather** - Face storms, environmental hazards, and seasonal challenges
- **Regular Content Updates** - We keep the server fresh with new events and features

### Getting Started

1. **Install Icarus** from Epic Games Store (it's free!)
2. **Start the game** and select "Multiplayer"
3. **Add our server** using the IP below
4. **Create your character** and join the adventure!

### Survival Tips

- **Bring essentials** - Never venture out without food, water, and a weapon
- **Build a shelter** - Protect yourself from hostile creatures and weather
- **Join a faction** - Team up with other players for better resource sharing
- **Explore carefully** - The wilderness is beautiful but dangerous

### Server Rules

- **Team play encouraged** - Help each other succeed
- **No griefing** - Respect other players' bases and progress
- **Resource sharing** - Support newer players when you can
- **Keep it fun** - This is about adventure and community

### Available Seasons

Our server runs multiple concurrent seasons/maps, so you can always find fresh content to explore!
